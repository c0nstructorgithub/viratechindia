Professional Delayed-Data Analysis for Smarter Swing & Position Trading

MetaStock D/C is designed for end-of-day, swing and position traders of all experience levels. It uses XENITH for Daily Charts to provide continuous delayed-data updates throughout the trading session.

What you can do

  • Chart virtually any market security
  • Scan markets to find opportunities matching your criteria
  • Backtest trading strategies and evaluate historical performance
  • Forecast future price movements based on historical patterns
  • Access expert, strategy-based advice integrated into your charts
  • Monitor portfolio lists and track instrument performance
  • Monitor Option Chains for sentiment analysis and risk plotting
  • Use industry-leading charting tools for detailed analysis

Markets covered: stocks, options, mutual funds, futures, commodities, FOREX, bonds and indices.

MetaStock D/C provides ready-to-use trading solutions out of the box, and every tool can be customised to match your individual trading style and strategy.
