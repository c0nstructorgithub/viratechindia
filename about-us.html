Viratech was formed over 2 decades ago with a clear focus to be a leader in the sphere of Technical Analysis. With a solid proven track record across the globe, and having equipped leading Banks, Institutions and thousands of Retail Traders & Investors,Viratech truly is a one stop shop to meet every possible Technical traders requirement. Viratech wrote history in the 90’s by bringing to India, both MetaStock and eSignal Advanced GET giving people the opportunity to use platforms that the world’s biggest and best traders use.

Today Viratech caters to every segment covering both domestic and international requirements. With an award winning product line Viratech offers products relating to both investing and trading, be it in equities, commodities, currencies and derivatives. The solutions give traders the power to access charts across multiple timeframes, both real time and end of day and are packaged with several value adds including Live news and fundamental data (*powered by Thomson Reuters). Besides the goal of providing world class software, data and training, our core focus has been to synergize this combination so that it results in long term sustainable profitable trading & investing. Viratech has set the standard for excellence in the industry in all these areas.

The commitment to training comes out of a passion for the subject of technical analysis and with each seminar or workshop that is periodically held, the content, approach and strategies are continuously upgraded with the sole objective of adapting technology to improve trading success. These workshops are led by Mr. Mohindar (Director) who is a seasoned technical analyst, active trader and has been regularly featured across leading media channels including CNN, CNBC, NDTV & ZEE Business. Mr. Mohindar has been a guest lecturer across renowned exchanges like the SGX, BSE and leading universities and business schools including the renowned IIM. Mr. Mohindar’s findings have also been covered in a number of leading websites, financial dailies and international tradeshows.

Not only has he spoken at multiple MetaStock Conferences incl USA, SE ASIA, INDIA and Australia - he was also rated as the #1 speaker at the prestigious MetaStock Conference at Las Vegas. Mr. Mohindar's interviews and techniques have been featured in leading publications and books including “Trading in a Nutshell” and “Secrets of Successful Trading”.

Today the RMO and RMO ATM have been the brain child of Viratech and in particular Mr. Rahul Mohindar. The RMO is built into MetaStock for several years now and continues to be to be one of the most popular systems in the product. The RMO ATM 2.0 also set a new industry standard when it came out with live scanning and talking voice alerts which reduce a traders dependency to be glued to a monitor.
