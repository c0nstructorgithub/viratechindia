  • RMO ATM 4.0

For over a decade, the RMO has been one of the most popular systems in MetaStock, and the RMO ATM add-on is the perfect extension of it. RMO ATM 4.0 drives an automated ATM PowerScreener that scans the markets in real time for trading setups and delivers live voice and email alerts, so you never miss a signal.

Version 4.0 introduces the all-new ATM Trend Flare studies, designed to detect emerging trends while prioritising volatility considerations. The system works across multiple timeframes and asset classes, and can be fully customised to suit your trading style.

9 Core Methods

  • ATM Breakout Catcher — solves the direction puzzle by auto-interpreting whether the market is in a Bullish, Bearish or Neutral phase, using volume and volatility through its ATM ZONE detector.
  • ATM SWI (Strength Weakness Index) — uses volume as its core ingredient to measure trend strength and weakness, giving you an excellent gauge of the strength and weakness of the symbols you trade.
  • ATM RMO SuperFilter — a refined and adaptive take on the classic RMO system that shades the trend into four colours, helping you detect direction and pulse the strength of the trend.
  • ATM TrendDecider Suite — plots a single level of support and resistance on your price chart for Daily, Weekly and Monthly periods, helping you determine momentum shifts.
  • ATM RMO II — helps you understand where the market has corrected within an established trend, enabling you to buy Calls in a falling market and Put Options in a rising market.
  • ATM Counter Trend Indicator — automatically stamps Buy and Sell signals on the charts based on price and volume breakouts.
  • ATM Trend Flare – Normal — the new, responsive Trend Flare study that detects emerging trends quickly while accounting for volatility, marking entries and trailing stops as it goes.
  • ATM Trend Flare – Slow — the data-heavy companion to Normal mode; the two work cohesively to give trading opportunities suited to your style, again marking entries and trailing stops.
  • ATM Auto Support and Resistance — an Expert Advisor that automatically marks out critical areas of support and resistance, so you can quickly identify potential spots where reversals and breakouts are likely to occur.
